Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2025

Who we are

SubID Matrix Tracker (“SMT”, “we”, “us”) helps creators and affiliates generate channel-tagged affiliate URLs and export CSVs locally in the browser. Contact: support@subidmx.com.

What this product does

SMT runs locally in your browser. You paste your URLs and channels; we transform links and generate a CSV download. The list of URLs you paste is not sent to our servers.

Information we process

  • Payments — processed by FastSpring (our authorized reseller). We do not receive or store card details.
  • Operational contact data — if you provide an email during purchase to receive the receipt or support.
  • Basic technical logs — high-level, aggregate site metrics for reliability and abuse prevention. No marketing tracking pixels are used.

Cookies & analytics

We do not set marketing cookies. FastSpring may set strictly necessary cookies to operate checkout and comply with tax or fraud controls.

Retention
